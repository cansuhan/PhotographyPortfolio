About Me

I am an amateur photographer.
I've been wanting to build my own photography website for a while and found Code First Girls!

The video below is from when I was working as an archaeologist in Cyprus.
The pandemic disrupted excavations and jobs in archaeology, so I decided to embark on a journey with coding and currently I am a sofware engineer apprentice at BAE Systems Digital Intelligence.
